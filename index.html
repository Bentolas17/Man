Forged From Experience

IRONVEIL started in Cagayan de Oro in 1994 as a small steel trading outfit. Today we supply and fabricate structural steel, ready-mix concrete, and precast components for the country's largest infrastructure programs.

Every product we ship meets ASTM, AISC, and DPWH standards — no shortcuts, no compromise.
